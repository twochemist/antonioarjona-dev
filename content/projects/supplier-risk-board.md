Supplier Risk Board is a portfolio web app for industrial supplier risk tracking. It is designed like an internal operations dashboard for manufacturing, quality and supplier industrialization teams.

The app tracks supplier issues such as injection molding defects, dimensional deviations, delivery delays, GP12 containment, automation downtime and quality spills. It includes KPI cards, risk and status filters, due-date sorting and a local create-issue workflow using mock manufacturing data.

Built with `Next.js`, `TypeScript`, `Tailwind CSS` and local React state as a focused version 1 without backend complexity.
